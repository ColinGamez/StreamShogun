# Account & Login

An account is **optional** — StreamShōgun works fully offline without one. An account is needed for Cloud Sync (Pro) and subscription management.

## Creating an Account

1. Go to **Settings** (⚙️) → **Account**.
2. Click **"Sign Up"**.
3. Enter your email and choose a password.
4. Click **"Create Account"**.

## Logging In

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**.
2. Click **"Log In"**.
3. Enter your email and password.
4. Click **"Log In"**.

You'll stay logged in across app restarts until you explicitly log out or your session expires.

## Logging Out

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**.
2. Click **"Log Out"**.

This clears your local session. Your cloud data (if any) remains on the server.

## Forgot Password

Password reset is handled via the web portal:
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login screen.
2. Check your email for a reset link.
3. Follow the link to set a new password.
4. Log in with your new password.

## Session & Security

- Sessions use secure refresh tokens with automatic rotation.
- Sessions expire after a set period for security.
- You can log out from all devices by changing your password.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| "Invalid email or password" | Double-check your credentials. Use "Forgot Password" if unsure. |
| Session keeps expiring | This is normal security behaviour. Log in again. If it happens very frequently, contact support. |
| Can't create account | Ensure your email is valid and not already registered. |
| Not receiving reset email | Check spam/junk folders. The email comes from noreply@streamshogun.com. |

## When to Contact Support

If you're locked out of your account and password reset doesn't work:

📧 **support@streamshogun.com**
