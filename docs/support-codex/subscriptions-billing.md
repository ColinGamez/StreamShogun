# Subscriptions & Billing

StreamShōgun offers a **Free** tier and a **Pro** subscription with additional features.

## Plans

### Free Plan
- Unlimited playlists and channels
- Full playback with custom controls
- EPG / programme guide
- Watch history
- Channel search and group filters

### Pro Plan
- Everything in Free, plus:
- **Picture-in-Picture (PIP)** mini player
- **Cloud Sync** — sync settings, favourites, and history across devices
- Priority support
- Annual billing saves you money

## How to Upgrade to Pro

1. Go to **Settings** (⚙️) → **Account**.
2. Click **"Upgrade to Pro"**.
3. Choose **Monthly** or **Annual** billing.
4. Complete payment via Stripe (secure checkout).
5. Your Pro features activate immediately.

You can also upgrade from the **Upgrade** banner that appears in the app.

## Managing Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**.
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"**.
3. This opens the Stripe Customer Portal where you can:
   - View your current plan
   - Update payment method
   - Download invoices
   - Switch between monthly and annual billing

## Cancelling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account** → **"Manage Subscription"**.
2. In the Stripe portal, click **"Cancel plan"**.
3. Your Pro features remain active until the end of your current billing period.
4. After that, you'll revert to the Free plan.

No data is lost when downgrading — your playlists and channels remain intact.

## Common Issues

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Payment failed | Check your card details in the Stripe portal. Try a different payment method. |
| Pro features not activating | Log out and back in to refresh your entitlements. |
| Charged after cancellation | Cancellations take effect at the end of the billing period. If you believe you were incorrectly charged, contact support. |
| Can't find "Manage Subscription" | Ensure you're logged in to the same account that has the subscription. |

## Refund Policy

Please contact support for refund requests within 14 days of purchase.
